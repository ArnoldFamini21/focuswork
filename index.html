Our Story

Why We Created Focus Work

Focus Work was founded by people with lived experience of neurodivergence.

We know how long the assessment process can take. Months waiting for referrals followed by more months waiting for medication reviews. During that time, you are still expected to perform at work as if nothing is happening.

You are told help is coming, but you are left managing deadlines, meetings, overwhelm, and anxiety on your own.

We created Focus Work to bridge that gap.

You should not have to wait for a diagnosis or medication to start building skills that make work manageable. Coaching gives you practical strategies now. Systems you can use immediately. Tools that reduce stress while you navigate the clinical process.

We hand-picked our coaches not just for qualifications, but for how they made us feel in sessions:

Supported Understood Not Judged Entirely at Ease Clear About What to Do Next

Every coach delivers structured, strategy-led sessions designed to produce real, measurable outcomes. Not vague encouragement or surface-level advice.

Our aim is to help capable people build the skills and systems they need to thrive at work or when starting their own business.

You do not need to wait to start building these skills.
