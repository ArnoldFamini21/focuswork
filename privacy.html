When you contact us, book a consultation, or engage in coaching services, we may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and any information you choose to share about your workplace challenges.

We process your data under UK GDPR on the basis of consent, contractual necessity, and legitimate interest. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations.

You have the right to request access to your data, request corrections, request deletion, or withdraw consent at any time.
